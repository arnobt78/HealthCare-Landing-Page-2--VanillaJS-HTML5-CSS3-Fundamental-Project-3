Daily Prevention Framework

Preventive care is not one big action. It is a set of repeatable routines that keep risk low and help detect issues early.

  • Track blood pressure, blood sugar, and weight trends monthly.
  • Schedule age-appropriate screening tests and annual physicals.
  • Prioritize sleep quality, hydration, and balanced nutrition.
  • Follow a realistic weekly activity goal that you can sustain.

Consistency beats intensity. Small habits done weekly are more effective than occasional extreme changes.
